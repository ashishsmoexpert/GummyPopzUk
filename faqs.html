ABOUT THE PRODUCT

Gummi Popz is a first-of-its-kind, multi-sensory popping gummy candy. Each individual Gummi Popz has three components working in explosive harmony: a soft, chewy gummi outside, a flavoured candy gel inside, and amazing poppin' sugar crystals on top. You Fill it, Gel it, Top it, and Pop it — however you want. No rules. Just wild combos.
Two flavours are currently available in the UK: Gummi Popz Sweet (Wild Berry) — sweet, chewy gummi madness with mixed berry flavour and sweet candy gel; and Gummi Popz Sour (Citrus Sour) — a sour citrus explosion with sour candy gel and poppin' citrus crystals. New flavours are in development — sign up to our newsletter to be first in the know.
Simple. Chaotic. Amazing. Fill the gummi with the candy gel (or squeeze it in, or go full send). Add the poppin' crystals on top (or load them inside). Then pop the whole thing in your mouth and brace for impact. The popping, crackling, chewy chaos hits you in waves. Every bite is a mini OMG moment. Or build it your way — there are no rules.
Please refer to the product packaging for full ingredient and allergen information. Contact us if you have specific dietary requirements and we'll be happy to help.
Gummi Popz is recommended for ages 4 and above. As with all confectionery, young children should be supervised when eating. Parental discretion is advised for very young children due to the popping candy element.
