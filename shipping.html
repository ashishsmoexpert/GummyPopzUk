Shipping Information

Everything you need to know about delivery within the UK

We deliver Gummi Popz nationwide across the United Kingdom. Below you'll find all the details about our shipping options, costs, and delivery timeframes.

UK Delivery Options

We offer the following delivery services for consumer orders placed through our website:

Service Cost Estimated Delivery
Standard Delivery
Royal Mail 48		 £3.95 2–4 working days

Royal Mail 24		 £5.95 1–2 working days
Next Working Day
DPD Express		 £8.95 Next working day (order by 2pm)

Free Delivery

We offer FREE Standard Delivery on all orders over £30.00. This will be automatically applied at checkout — no discount code needed.

Processing Time

Orders are processed and dispatched Monday to Friday (excluding bank holidays). Orders placed before 2pm on a working day are typically dispatched the same day. Orders placed after 2pm, or on weekends/bank holidays, will be dispatched the next working day.

Delivery Areas

We currently ship to the following UK destinations:

  • England, Scotland, Wales: All standard and express services available
  • Northern Ireland: Standard delivery only (2–5 working days)
  • Scottish Highlands & Islands, Isle of Man, Scilly Isles: Standard delivery only (3–7 working days). Additional delivery charge may apply for remote areas — we'll contact you before dispatch if this affects your order.

We do not currently ship to international destinations or to PO boxes.

Tracking Your Order

Once your order has been dispatched, you'll receive a shipping confirmation email containing your tracking number and a link to track your parcel. Express and Next Day orders receive full tracking updates via SMS/email from DPD.

Packaging

All orders are carefully packaged to ensure your Gummi Popz arrive in perfect condition. We use protective packaging to prevent damage during transit. Our packaging materials are recyclable wherever possible.

Issues With Delivery

If you experience any issues with your delivery, please contact us as soon as possible:

  • Missing or delayed parcel: Contact us at hello@gummipopzuk.com with your order number
  • Damaged goods: Please take photos of the damaged packaging/products and email us immediately
  • Wrong address: Contact us before dispatch to update your delivery address. Once dispatched, we cannot redirect parcels

Wholesale Orders

Wholesale and trade orders have different shipping terms. For trade delivery information, please see our Wholesale page or contact our trade team directly.

Returns & Refunds

For information about returns and refunds, please see our Terms & Conditions or contact our customer service team.
